The numbers used for intergalactic transactions follows a similar convention to the roman numerals.

I have painstakingly collected the appropriate translation between roman numerals and arabic numerals to facilitate this process.

Guide: Credits

Metal & Dirt Values

To save you some time, I have pre-saved TWO options for validating your Metal inputs and of course, I made sure our valuable 'Dirt' is included! Remember, the shorter your list, the faster your computations will be. Thus, I have allowed a third option by which you can add your own list of metals (don't forget the Dirt if you need it!), and a fourth option where you leave it up to me to try and decipher your metal and dirt needs... I know you don't always have to time to verify your metals yourself.

1.) (pre-saved) shortlist containing dirt and metals commonly used: Gold, Silver, Iron, Dirt.

2.) (pre-saved) complete list of metals found on Earth's Periodic Table of Elements, plus Dirt. [source]

3.) (input required) upload a .txt file containing your own list of metals/dirt for validation. Make sure each is listed on a new line!

4.) I will try my best to guess and validate.
